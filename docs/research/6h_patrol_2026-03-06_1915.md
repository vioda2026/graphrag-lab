## B) 详尽结构化记录

### 1. Benchmark 进展

#### GraphRAG-Bench (ICLR'26)
- **论文**: "When to use Graphs in RAG: A Comprehensive Analysis for Graph Retrieval-Augmented Generation"
- **作者**: Xiang Zhishang, Wu Chuanjie, Zhang Qinggang, et al. (Xiamen University, PolyU)
- **评测数据集**:
  - GraphRAG-Bench (Novel): 文学/虚构内容
  - GraphRAG-Bench (Medical): 医疗/健康内容
- **任务难度分级**:
  | Level | 任务类型 | 示例 |
  |-------|----------|------|
  | 1 | Fact Retrieval | "Mont St. Michel 位于法国哪个地区？" |
  | 2 | Complex Reasoning | "Hinze 与 Felicia 的协议如何影响英格兰统治者的看法？" |
  | 3 | Contextual Summarization | "John Curgenven 作为康沃尔船夫的角色是什么？" |
  | 4 | Creative Generation | "将亚瑟王与 John Curgenven 的比较改写成新闻报道" |
- **评测指标**: Accuracy, ROUGE-L, Coverage, Factual Score
- **支持框架**: LightRAG, Fast GraphRAG, HippoRAG2, 等
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/GraphRAG-Bench/GraphRAG-Benchmark
- **HuggingFace**: https://huggingface.co/datasets/GraphRAG-Bench/GraphRAG-Bench

#### WildGraphBench (2026-02-03)
- **论文**: "WildGraphBench: Benchmarking GraphRAG with Wild-Source Corpora"
- **创新点**: 使用野生来源（非 curated）语料评测 GraphRAG
- **价值**: 更贴近真实场景，避免基准测试过拟合

### 2. 图结构创新

#### 2.1 HELP: HyperNode Expansion + Logical Path (2026-02-24)
- **论文**: "HELP: HyperNode Expansion and Logical Path-Guided Evidence Localization for Accurate and Efficient GraphRAG"
- **作者**: Yuqi Huang, Ning Liao, Kai Yang, et al.
- **核心策略**:
  1. **HyperNode Expansion**: 迭代链接知识三元组形成超节点
  2. **Logical Path-Guided Evidence Localization**: 逻辑路径引导证据定位
- **优势**: 平衡准确性与效率，减少图遍历开销

#### 2.2 Core-based Hierarchies (2026-03-05)
- **论文**: "Core-based Hierarchies for Efficient GraphRAG"
- **作者**: Jakir Hossain, Ahmet Erdem Sarıyüce
- **创新点**: 基于 core decomposition 的社区分解优化层级索引
- **优势**: 减少索引构建成本，提升检索效率

#### 2.3 Relink: Query-Driven Evidence Graph (2026-01-11)
- **论文**: "Relink: Constructing Query-Driven Evidence Graph On-the-Fly for GraphRAG"
- **作者**: Manzong Huang, Chenyang Bu, Yi He, et al.
- **创新点**: 动态构建查询驱动的证据图
- **优势**: 按需构建，减少预计算开销

### 3. 图探索策略创新

#### 3.1 GraphScout: Agentic Graph Reasoning (2026-03-01)
- **论文**: "GraphScout: Empowering Large Language Models with Intrinsic Exploration Ability for Agentic Graph Reasoning"
- **作者**: Yuchen Ying, Weiqi Jiang, Tongya Zheng, et al. (Zhejiang University)
- **核心创新**:
  - 赋予 LLM 内在探索能力
  - 解决现有方法依赖手动设计探索策略的问题
- **方法**: 迭代交互 + 自主探索

#### 3.2 A2RAG: Adaptive Agentic Graph Retrieval (2026-01-28)
- **论文**: "A2RAG: Adaptive Agentic Graph Retrieval for Cost-Aware and Reliable Reasoning"
- **作者**: Jiate Liu, Zebin Chen, Shaobo Qiao, et al.
- **核心策略**:
  - **自适应控制器**: 验证证据充分性，仅在必要时触发细化
  - **成本感知**: 平衡推理质量与计算成本
- **优势**: 减少不必要的图探索开销

#### 3.3 GraphSearch: Agentic Deep Searching (2026-09-26)
- **论文**: "GraphSearch: An Agentic Deep Searching Workflow for Graph Retrieval-Augmented Generation"
- **作者**: Cehao Yang, Xiaojun Wu, Xueyuan Lin, et al.
- **创新点**: 智能体深度搜索工作流
- **优势**: 结构化建模知识，支持复杂推理

### 4. 效率优化

#### 4.1 LinearRAG (ICLR'26)
- **论文**: "LinearRAG: Linear Graph Retrieval Augmented Generation on Large-scale Corpora"
- **作者**: Luyao Zhuang, Shengyuan Chen, Yilin Xiao, et al.
- **核心创新**: 无关系图构建（relation-free）
- **优势**: 线性时间复杂度，适合大规模语料

#### 4.2 Democratizing GraphRAG / SPRIG (2026-02-26)
- **论文**: "Democratizing GraphRAG: Linear, CPU-Only Graph Retrieval for Multi-Hop QA"
- **作者**: Qizhi Wang
- **核心创新**: SPRIG (Seeded Propagation for Retrieval In Graphs)
- **特点**:
  - CPU-only
  - 线性时间
  - 无 token 消耗
- **优势**: 降低 GraphRAG 使用门槛

#### 4.3 MOBIMEM (2025-12-15)
- **论文**: "Beyond Training: Enabling Self-Evolution of Agents with MOBIMEM"
- **作者**: Zibin Liu, Cheng Zhang, Xi Zhao, et al.
- **性能**: 280x faster than GraphRAG baselines, 9x latency reduction
- **创新点**: 任务无关的插件记忆模块

### 8. 重要发现与趋势

1. **GraphRAG 有效性条件**：
   - 在层级知识检索场景优于传统 RAG
   - 在深度上下文推理任务表现更佳
   - 在简单事实检索任务可能不如传统 RAG

2. **评估趋势**：
   - 从单一 Accuracy 向多维度评估转变
   - 强调事实一致性 (Factual Score)
   - 关注幻觉检测 (Hallucination Detection)

3. **技术趋势**：
   - 效率优化（LinearRAG, SPRIG, MOBIMEM）
   - 领域应用（医疗、工业、教育）
   - 自适应/成本感知推理（A2RAG）
